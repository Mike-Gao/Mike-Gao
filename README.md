I was born and reared in Shanghai, China before moving to the United States to attend a preparatory boarding school in the United States. I currently reside in Montreal, Canada. 

I have long been drawn to Computer Science because of its powerful interdisciplinary potential. The computing industry is the only frontier which inspires unity between otherwise disparate areas of science and allows for objective analysis of society’s most pressing problems. I believe that harnessing the power of technology is the most efficient way to discern optimal solutions to real world issues.

Within the field of Computer Graphics and Machine Learning, I'm especially interested in the study of rendering techniques, denoising methods and artistic style transfer.
